# 🌍 East Africa Electronics: Strategic Sales Analysis (3.6B KSh)

### 📈 Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset of **3.6 Billion KSh** in electronics sales across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. The objective was to evaluate market performance, identify "Profit Stars," and provide data-driven recommendations for regional expansion.

### 📊 Master KPIs (KSh)
* **Total Revenue:** 3.61 Billion KSh
* **Total Profit:** 2.41 Billion KSh
* **Overall Portfolio Margin:** 66.6%
* **Market Hub:** Kenya (38% of regional profit)

### 💡 High-Impact Insights
1. **The Efficiency Champion:** While **Macbooks** drive the highest revenue (over 1 Billion KSh), **Airpods** are the most efficient product with a **74.7% Profit Margin**. 
2. **Regional Leadership:** **Kenya** is the primary profit driver. Strategic focus should remain on strengthening the "In-Store" presence in Kenya to anchor the regional business.
3. **Optimizing Rwanda:** The **iPad** is underperforming in Rwanda with a 57% margin. Recommendation: Audit import duties or shipping logistics to bring Rwanda closer to the 66% regional average.

### 🛠️ Methodology
* **Scale Normalization:** Adjusted unit prices to reflect real-world Kenyan market values (e.g., Macbooks at 129,900 KSh).
* **Feature Engineering:** Calculated `Profit Margin %` to distinguish between high-volume "Cash Cows" and high-efficiency "Stars."
* **Visualizations:** Created revenue-vs-efficiency scatter plots to visualize product portfolio health.
